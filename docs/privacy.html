What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use the BeerMate app and related services. By using BeerMate, you agree to the practices described here and in our separate Terms of Use.

This page is written in clear language so App Store reviewers and users can quickly understand:

  • what personal data we collect,
  • why we collect it,
  • how long we keep it, and
  • what choices and rights you have.
03

Data we collect

3.1 Information you provide directly

When you interact with BeerMate, you may provide:

  • Account details – such as email address, username, and password (or authentication provider details, if we support sign-in with third-party services).
  • Profile & preferences – your preferred styles, taste profile, and in-app settings.
  • Beer activity – ratings, reviews, wishlists, favorites, check-ins, and tasting notes.
  • Feedback & support – messages you send us, bug reports, and other communication.
  • Optional media – photos of beers, cans, or bottles you upload if that feature is enabled.

3.2 Information collected automatically

When you use BeerMate, certain information is collected automatically to keep the app running smoothly:

  • Usage data – screens viewed, features used, simple interaction analytics (e.g., how often you rate beers or open the app).
  • Device & technical data – device type, operating system, app version, language, and anonymized identifiers used for diagnostics.
  • Log & diagnostics data – crash logs, performance data, and error traces, so we can identify and fix issues.
  • Approximate location (if permitted) – used for region-aware experiences such as surfacing breweries in your country or region. We do not require precise GPS location for basic use.

3.3 Data we do not intentionally collect

We do not intentionally collect or store:

  • Government identification numbers.
  • Precise geolocation unless you explicitly grant that permission.
  • Payment card numbers (in-app purchases, if any, are handled by Apple/Google).
  • Sensitive health or medical records.
If you believe we have collected data we should not have (for example, because it was accidentally included in a screenshot or note), please contact us and we will work with you to remove it.
04

How we use your data

We use the data described above for the following purposes:

  • Operating the app – allowing you to create an account, sign in, view your history, and use all core BeerMate features.
  • Personalization – recommending beers and breweries based on your ratings, wishlists, and taste profile.
  • Analytics & improvements – understanding how features are used, improving recommendations, stabilizing performance, and guiding roadmap decisions.
  • Safety & abuse prevention – detecting suspicious patterns, preventing spam or misuse, and enforcing our Terms of Use.
  • Communication – sending important notices about changes to this policy, security updates, or critical app changes. We do not spam users with unrelated marketing.
We may use aggregated or anonymized data (that cannot reasonably identify you) for analytics, product improvement, and high-level statistics. We do not sell your personal data.

If you are in the EU, UK, or a jurisdiction with similar privacy laws, we process your personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Contract – to provide the BeerMate app and features you choose to use.
  • Legitimate interests – to keep the app secure, improve functionality, and understand usage patterns, where those interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Consent – for certain optional features (for example, specific analytics or notifications) where required by local law. Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Legal obligations – to comply with laws, regulations, or valid legal requests.

How and when we share your data

We limit sharing and only disclose your personal data in these circumstances:

  • Service providers – trusted third parties who host our infrastructure, store data, provide analytics, logging, or crash reporting. They process data on our behalf and under contractual obligations to protect it.
  • Legal reasons – if we must respond to valid legal requests, enforce our Terms, or protect the rights, property, or safety of BeerMate, our users, or others.
  • Business transfers – if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to similar protections.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their independent marketing.

Retention, security & underage users

7.1 How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide BeerMate, support the features you use, and comply with legal obligations. Factors that influence retention include:

  • Whether you maintain an active account.
  • Whether we need certain records to resolve disputes or troubleshoot issues.
  • Legal, regulatory, or tax requirements that apply to us.

If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize personal data where reasonably possible, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain information.

7.2 Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No system can be perfectly secure, but we design BeerMate with security best practices in mind and review our approach periodically.

7.3 Underage users

BeerMate is only for users who are at or above the legal drinking age in their country, province, or state. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals who do not meet this requirement.

If you believe someone under the legal drinking age is using BeerMate, please contact us so we can remove their access and delete associated data where appropriate.
